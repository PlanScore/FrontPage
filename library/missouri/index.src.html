Redistrict Plans: Missouri

Missouri’s congressional districts are drawn by the state legislature, as a regular statute, subject to gubernatorial veto. As of the 2020 vote, the state legislative lines are drawn by two separate politician commissions — one for state Senate districts, one for state House districts.
