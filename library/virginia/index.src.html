Redistricting Plans: Virginia

In Virginia, redistricting plans are created by a sixteen-member hybrid political commission made up of 8 politicians and 8 citizens. The final plans are enacted by the legislature which does not have the power amend the plan submitted by the commission. Democrats control the legislature.
