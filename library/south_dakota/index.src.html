Redistricting Plans: South Dakota

In South Dakota, redistricting plans for the state legislature are enacted by the legislature and are subject to gubernatorial veto. Republicans control the legislature and the Governor's office. South Dakota has only one seat in the House of Representatives, therefore there is no congressional redistricting.
