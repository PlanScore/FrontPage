Redistricting Plans: Wisconsin

In Wisconsin, redistricting plans are enacted by the legislature and are subject to gubernatorial veto. For the 2020 redistricting cycle, the Governor created by executive order a citizen redistricting commission to submit maps for the legislature's consideration. Republicans control the legislature and the Governor is a Democrat.
