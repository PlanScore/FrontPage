Redistricting Plans: Arkansas

In Arkansas, congressional districts are adopted by the legislature and are subject to gubernatorial veto. State legislative districts are drawn by a political commission composed of the Governor, Secretary of State, and the Attorney General. Republicans control the legislature, and all three members of the state political commission are Republican.
